Voice-Driven Capture

NOTA is voice notes, the app.

NOTA is the rebranded voice-driven capture product. The job is simple and clear: talk into it, capture the idea, keep the note, and turn spoken fragments into usable text. It is not trying to be Magnum Opus. It is fast, lightweight, and centered on voice.
